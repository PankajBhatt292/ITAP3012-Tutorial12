Our Values

Mission:

Provide people with high quality education and training, giving them life transforming knowledge and skills

Vision:

VIT aims to create education networks of excellence for lifelong learning; where people come to learn, grow and achieve

What our values mean to us

Values Its Meaning
Students first Focus first on providing quality services to our students. We place the student experience at the core of all we do
Integrity Do the right thing at all times and in all circumstances, whether or not anyone is watching
Teamwork and ownership Act on behalf of the entire company. We work together effectively to achieve VIT succes
Innovate and simplify Look towards innovation and always find ways to simplify. Be externally aware and look for new ideas
Quality focused We take pride in providing high value services to our students, which ensures their satisfaction and our future
