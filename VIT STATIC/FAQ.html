Knowing the average living costs in Australia is an important part of your financial preparation. The Department of Home Affairs has financial requirements you must meet in order to receive a student visa for Australia. As of October 2019, the 12-month living costs are AUD$21,041 for students or guardians, AUD$7,362 for partners coming with you, and AUD$3,152 for a child coming with you. Please refer to the link below for more information about cost of living in Australia:https://www.studyinaustralia.gov.au/English/Live-in-Australia/living-costs
